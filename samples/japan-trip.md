# Spring in Japan — 10 day itinerary (2 travelers)

## Day 1 — Arrival
Land at Narita Airport in the afternoon. Take the Narita Express into Tokyo
Station, check in near Marunouchi.

## Day 2-3 — Tokyo
Two full days exploring Tokyo: Senso-ji, Meiji Shrine, Shibuya, teamLab.
Getting around by metro all day.

## Day 4 — Day trip to Hakone
Morning Romancecar from Shinjuku to Hakone-Yumoto for onsen and the open-air
museum. Return to Shinjuku in the evening on the Romancecar.

## Day 5 — To Kyoto
Shinkansen from Tokyo to Kyoto (Nozomi, reserved seats). Evening stroll in Gion.

## Day 6-7 — Kyoto & Nara
Day 6: temples in Kyoto by bus. Day 7: morning train to Nara (JR) to see the
deer and Todai-ji, back to Kyoto the same evening.

## Day 8 — Hiroshima
Early Nozomi from Kyoto to Hiroshima. Afternoon at the Peace Memorial Park.
Local train to Miyajimaguchi and the ferry over to Miyajima for sunset,
then back to Hiroshima for the night.

## Day 9 — Osaka
Shinkansen from Hiroshima to Shin-Osaka. Dotonbori food crawl in the evening.

## Day 10 — Departure
Haruka airport express from Kansai Airport... wait, the other direction:
from Kyoto? No — we fly out of KIX, so take the Nankai rapi:t from Namba to
Kansai Airport in the morning.
